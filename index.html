Have questions about your travel plans? Find answers to common traveler FAQs here

You typically need a valid passport, visa (if applicable), travel insurance, and sometimes vaccination certificates, depending on the destination.

  • Passport with 6+ months validity
  • Tourist or Business Visa
  • Travel Insurance
  • COVID-19/Vaccination Certificates
  • Hotel Bookings
  • Return Tickets

Stay hydrated, wear comfortable clothing, bring entertainment, and move around periodically to avoid fatigue and jet lag.

  • Neck Pillow & Eye Mask
  • Noise-Canceling Headphones
  • Compression Socks
  • Healthy Snacks
  • Download Movies/Books
  • Hydration Tablets

Use local ATMs for the best exchange rates, or bring an international debit/credit card with no foreign transaction fees.

  • Use ATMs at major banks
  • Check currency conversion fees
  • Notify your bank before travel
  • Use multi-currency travel cards
  • Avoid airport exchange booths
  • Keep some local cash handy

Stay aware of your surroundings, avoid isolated areas at night, keep copies of your documents, and use registered transport.

  • Keep emergency contacts handy
  • Share your itinerary with someone
  • Secure your valuables
  • Use hotel safes
  • Install a travel safety app
  • Buy a local SIM card or eSIM
